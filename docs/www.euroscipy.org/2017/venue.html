Venue

The conference is held in the Friedrich-Alexander University Erlangen-Nürnberg, or FAU for short.

Address

Friedrich-Alexander-Universität Erlangen-Nürnberg
Physics department
Staudtstraße 5 91058 Erlangen

See also the location at Google Maps

The beginner tutorials will be in lecture room (Hörsaal in German) F, and the advanced tutorials in lecture room G. The conference talks will be in lecture room G. The sprints will be in seminar rooms 00.732 and 00.103.

Transportation

  • Train: Erlangen main station
  • Plane: The nearest international airport is located in Nuremberg which is only 16km from Erlangen.
