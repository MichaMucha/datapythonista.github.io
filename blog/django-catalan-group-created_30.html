Django catalan group created

Marc | Sun 30 September 2007

A new Django community has been created for Django users/developers that speak Catalan, or live in Catalonia.

Main reasons for its creation are:

  • Provide a resource where Catalan users can ask django questions in their own language.
  • Coordinate Django Catalan specific jobs, mostly Catalan translation (that now is up to date).
  • Be in touch with other Django community members in our little country (for business, beers...).
Want to join us? http://groups.google.com/group/django-cat
